IAM - It is stand for the identity and access manager 
IAM is used to restrict the unauthorized user to access the resources in cloud provider like AWS resources,Azure resources and GCP resources.


Example : Like the authorized person can come in office and work outsider can not work and enter.

Authentication and Authorization

Authentication is like to come to office but you are in accounts department not authorized to work in operational teams work.
Authorization is the second step of authentication like first authenticate then work where you are authorized accounts team can work in only accounts system not in another one.
